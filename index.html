HISTORY OF THE ADWS?! 📖 SERIES


The America:Divided We stand?! series, was created by the activist, and published and performance poet Leon Kwasi Kuntuo-Asare as a literary and photographic form of protest and activism. The series utilzies poetry, photography and essays as artistic weapons to fight against political, social and racial injustices.


The series uses those various forms of artistic expression and media to educate people within and outside of the African diaspora about history, politics, human sexuality, and social issues. Through art and creative writing, the ADWS?! series advocates for progressive reforms to fight against systematic White supremacy, economic injustice, and social inequalities in America and beyond 🌍 🌎 🌏 ...
